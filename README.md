# Salem Land Investment – Personal Notes

## General Behaviour

Most people prefer to buy land or plots **close to their home location**, mainly:

* for convenience of management
* due to ignorance or lack of knowledge about other localities

I have bought plots near my home. But wanted to explore high growth areas instead of clustering investments. 

---

## Approach

I went out of my way to identify the **best place to invest in my hometown, Salem**.

I roamed the city(~75kms per day) in my bike over the christmas holidays and visited multiple localities, contacted brokers, spoke to owners wherever possible.

I considered **the Bangalore-side direction** as a **high-growth corridor**, so I focused my search primarily there.

---

## Observation

Across the city, land is being quoted at around **₹4000 psf** for a “decent locality”, **irrespective of the actual importance or future potential of the area**.

Medium growth areas were quoted 2000+ psf.

Prime locality and posh localities were quoted 10,000 to 20,000 psf which is surprisingly priced at same level as bangalore.

This gives the advantage to play the realty game in Salem itself which is a known location instead of unknown location like bangalore.

Bangalore gives the advantage of rental yields. So, future investments must be focused here in future.

---

## Salem Master Plan 2041

During this exploration, I noticed **promoters hyping the Outer Ring Road**.

This led me to learn more about the Outer Ring Road and got to know about the **Salem Master Plan 2041**.

The document was a **very interesting read** and provided insights into:

* future development focus
* planned infrastructure and growth areas

### Traffic Observation

As expected, traffic volume on the **Attur side** is lower compared to:

* Coimbatore side
* Namakkal side

**Reference:**
Salem Master Plan – *Figure 59: A Capacity and Congestion Analysis*

## Identified High Growth Potential Areas

Based on the study:

* **Omalur**
  *(Science Park proposed, airport, future IT park proposals)*

* **Alagapuram Pudur + Gorimedu**
  *(Rapid growth, Narayana Techno & Chaitanya Techno schools)*

* **Karuppur**
  *(Nearest residential area to IT parks, 100-ft road connecting Yercaud)*

* **Steel Plant Road**
  *(Sports Centre proposal)*
